Biography

Akan Andy

With over 15 years of culinary experience, Chef Akan Andy brings passion, creativity, and expertise to the kitchen of our university cafeteria. Hailing from a family deeply rooted in the culinary arts, Chef Akan developed his love for cooking at a young age, inspired by the vibrant flavors and diverse cuisines of his heritage. Chef Akan began his culinary journey by attending a prestigious Culinary Institute, where he honed his skills and developed a strong foundation in classical culinary techniques. After graduation, he embarked on a culinary adventure, working in renowned restaurants across the country, each experience adding depth and dimension to his culinary repertoire.
